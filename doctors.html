GETTING ACQUAINTED
We love seeing new patients and are excited to talk with you about your family's dental care. We set aside time during your first visit to get to know one another, discuss your questions, and look at your long-term needs.

FIRST VISIT
Before your first visit, please check your insurance coverage. On your first visit, please come a little early to complete a patient information form and bring your insurance paperwork so we can help you get your dental benefits.

OUR APPROACH
Though we take our work very seriously, we pride ourselves on doing it in a fun way. We want you to enjoy coming to see us and to feel comfortable with the treatment you receive. We are always available to answer your questions and keep you updated on treatment progress.
